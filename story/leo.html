Reading Time

Leo and his Smartphone

Leo has a new smartphone. It is blue.

He plays video games on the phone. He is happy.

He watches funny videos on the phone. He laughs!

He calls his grandmother. He says, "Hello Grandma!"

Mom says, "Oh no! Leo, look at the time!"

But wait! It is 10 o'clock at night.

Leo is still using the phone.

The next day, Leo is very tired at school.

His mom says, "No more phone at night, Leo!"

Now Leo feels better.
