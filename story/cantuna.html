📖 Reading Time

Many years ago, Cantuña lived in Quito, Ecuador.


He was a young and talented builder.


He wanted to build a spectacular and giant church.


He worked hard every day with many big, heavy stones.


One day, Cantuña had a very big problem.


The sun was going down, and the church was not finished!


He was very worried and sad. He needed help fast!


A mysterious man appeared and offered help.


The mysterious man wanted to take Cantuña's soul if he finished the church.


But Cantuña was super clever!


He secretly hid one single stone under his coat.


Since one stone was missing, the work was not completed!


Cantuña saved himself and won!


🌟 The End 🌟
