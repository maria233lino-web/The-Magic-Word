☂️All about Ana☂️

A story about friendship, kindness and learning English.

Vocabulary

Coast

The land next to the sea or ocean.

Encebollado

A traditional Ecuadorian fish soup.

Bike

A vehicle with two wheels.

Student

A person who studies at school.

Warm-Up

Answer the following questions.

  • Do you have a bicycle?
  • What is your favorite color?
  • What traditional Ecuadorian food do you like?
  • What do you use when it rains?

📖 Reading Time

My name is Ana.

I am 9 years old.

I live in Guayaquil, on the coast of Ecuador.

I am a student.

I go to school every day because I like my school.

I have a small family.

My favorite color is blue.

I like chifles and hot encebollado.

I have a beautiful red bike.

I play with my family and I am happy.

I also like English because English is fun.

The End.
